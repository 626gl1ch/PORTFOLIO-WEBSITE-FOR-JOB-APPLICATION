ALMA Scalping Strategy

Quantitative Research Report v2.1

This document presents a high-frequency trading methodology leveraging Arnaud Legoux Moving Averages (ALMA). Unlike traditional SMA or EMA models, ALMA employs a Gaussian distribution to reduce lag while maintaining superior smoothness.

Technical Execution

The system utilizes a 5-minute primary bias confirmed by 1-minute execution triggers. Volume-weighted metrics ensure entry only during high-liquidity sessions.
